> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud Journey program. This will help the FCJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

Overall Evaluation

1. Working Environment
The working environment at FCJ is welcoming, supportive, and motivating. Everyone is approachable and willing to help whenever I encounter difficulties, which has given me more confidence throughout my internship. What I appreciate most is that the team encourages us to take the initiative to explore and learn instead of simply following instructions.

2. Support from Mentors and Team Admin
My mentor has always been patient, supportive, and dedicated throughout my learning journey. Rather than giving me direct answers, my mentor often guided me in the right direction and encouraged me to solve problems on my own. This approach has significantly improved my self-learning ability and problem-solving skills. The team admins have also been very responsive and helpful, ensuring that everything runs smoothly during the internship.

3. Relevance to My Academic Background
The tasks and learning materials provided during the program are closely related to my field of study and career goals. Besides reinforcing the knowledge I gained at university, I had the opportunity to explore many practical technologies and workflows that I had never experienced before. This internship has helped me bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications.

4. Learning Opportunities and Skill Development
This is the aspect I value the most. Every week brought new challenges and learning opportunities, from AWS services and DevOps practices to professional working skills. Although I faced many difficulties along the way, I always tried my best to research documentation, learn independently, and complete every assigned task. Looking back, I can clearly see how much I have improved—not only in technical knowledge but also in critical thinking, self-learning, and responsibility.

5. Company Culture and Team Spirit
One of the things that impressed me the most was the strong sense of teamwork within FCJ. Everyone is willing to share knowledge, exchange ideas, and support one another without hesitation. This positive culture has inspired me to keep improving myself and motivated me to contribute more whenever possible.

6. Internship Policies and Benefits
The program provides valuable learning opportunities through workshops, documentation, and continuous guidance from mentors. For me, the greatest benefit is not only the knowledge I have gained but also the practical experience and professional mindset that will help me in my future career.

Additional Questions

What was the thing you were most satisfied with during your internship?
The thing I appreciated the most was the opportunity to learn in a professional environment where I could actively explore new technologies, take on real tasks, and receive valuable guidance from mentors and other team members. Compared to when I first joined the program, I feel that I have grown significantly in both technical skills and confidence.

What do you think could be improved for future interns?
Overall, I think the program is already well organized. If possible, I would love to see more technical sharing sessions or cross-team discussions where interns can exchange experiences, learn from one another, and gain broader perspectives.

Would you recommend this internship program to your friends? Why?
Absolutely. I would highly recommend First Cloud Journey to anyone who is passionate about learning and developing practical skills. The program provides not only technical knowledge but also valuable experience in problem-solving, teamwork, and professional working practices.

Suggestions and Future Expectations

Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?
I hope the program can include even more real-world projects and regular feedback sessions. Receiving periodic evaluations would help interns better understand their strengths, identify areas for improvement, and set clearer goals for their personal development.

Would you like to continue this program in the future?
Yes, definitely. I sincerely hope to have the opportunity to continue my journey with First Cloud Journey. There is still so much for me to learn, and I believe I can continue growing both personally and professionally through this program. If given the chance, I will continue to work hard, stay proactive, and contribute more to the team.

Additional Comments
I would like to express my sincere gratitude to all the mentors, team admins, and everyone at First Cloud Journey for their continuous support and encouragement throughout my internship. This has been a truly meaningful experience for me. I will continue learning, improving myself, and I genuinely hope to have the opportunity to remain a part of the First Cloud Journey community in the future.
